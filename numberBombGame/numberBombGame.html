Summary

  • Number Bomb is a 2 player game
  • A bomb will be hidden behind a number ranging from 1 - 100
  • Players will take turns selecting a number
  • The goal is to avoid the bomb

How to play

  • Slide the white circle to select a number
  • You win if the other player selects the bomb
